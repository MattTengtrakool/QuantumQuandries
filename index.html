Quantum Quandaries

How to Play

Players alternate turns, each turn consists of placing a chip on any empty cell and then drawing a Quantum card.

The objective of the game is to control as many rows, columns or diagonals as possible. A player controls a line if they have more chips in it than their opponent.

Quantum Cards

After placing a chip, players draw a Quantum card. They can choose to play it immediately or save it for a future turn. Players can only hold up to 3 Quantum cards in their hand.

The game ends when all chips have been placed on the board or when the Quantum deck runs out. The player who controls the most lines is the winner.
